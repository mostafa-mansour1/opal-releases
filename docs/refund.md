## Refund eligibility

All sales of OPAL Pro are final. Due to the digital nature of the product, purchases are generally non-refundable once the license key has been delivered.

## Exceptions

Refund requests may be considered at our sole discretion in circumstances such as:

- The application fails to install or launch on a supported operating system and the issue cannot be resolved through support
- A duplicate charge occurred due to a payment processing error

Submitting a refund request does not guarantee approval. Each request is reviewed individually based on the details provided.

## How to submit a request

Email [contact@opalapi.dev](mailto:contact@opalapi.dev) with your Gumroad order number and a description of the issue. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

## Effect of refund

In the event a refund is approved, the associated license is immediately void. Continued use of OPAL Pro following a refund constitutes a breach of the [Terms of Use](https://opalapi.dev/terms).

## Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in permanent termination of your license and account. We encourage all disputes to be resolved directly.

## Free tier

The Free tier requires no payment and is not subject to this policy.
